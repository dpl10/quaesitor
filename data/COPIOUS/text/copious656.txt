Genus EPIMYS Trouessart.
1881.
Epimys Trouessart, Bull.
Soc. etudes Sci. dangers, 10, 117.
Type.
— Mus rattus Linnaeus.
True rats.
Size and appearance variable, from small soft-haired mouse-like species to large coarse-furred rats, larger than the Norway rat.
Several species have spines, mixed with the fur of the back, in certain pelages.
Tail usually little furred, scaly and relatively long.
Molars 33, slightly graduated in size from first to third.
