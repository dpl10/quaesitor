Konigl.
Preuss.
Akad.
Wiss, 703.
Type.
— Vespertilio pachypus Temminck.
Very small bats (head and body about 40 millimeters; tail about 25) with head greatly flattened (depth of brain case barely one-half of mastoid width); ears normal, with small tragus; color golden-brown.
